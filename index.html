Elon Musk

https://twitter.com/elonmusk
https://www.facebook.com/ermusk/

He is a Canadian-American engineer, businessman, inventor and investor, a dollar billionaire. He likes to participate in projects that change the world. He considers the colonization of Mars as one of his most important goals. Musk is also involved in developing a high-speed transportation system known as Hyperloop.

Experience

1995 - Founded the company called Zip2

Zip2 was a company that provided and licensed online city guide software to newspapers

1999 - Co-founded X.com

An X.com acquisition the following year led to the creation of PayPal as it is known today, and in October 2002, PayPal was acquired by eBay for $1.5 billion in stock

2002 - Founded SpaceX

SpaceX’s intention is to revolutionize space technology, with the ultimate goal of enabling people to live on other planets

2003 - Co-founder, CEO and product architect at Tesla Motors

A company dedicated to producing affordable, mass-market electric cars as well as battery products and solar roofs

2013 - Founded Neuralink

Neuralink is an neurotechnology company, reported to be developing implantable brain–computer interfaces (BCIs)

2015 - Co-founded OpenAI

OpenAI is a non-profit AI research company, discovering and enacting the path to safe artificial general intelligence
